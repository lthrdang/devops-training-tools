Day-10 Workflow

What to do during and after the capstone.

1

Open the dashboard before the session

Review the trainee's pre-test result and chapter completions so you know which areas to probe deeper.

2

Run the capstone with the rubric open

Score each skill area as the trainee demonstrates it. The rubric saves to localStorage automatically — refresh-safe.

3

Set the final recommendation

Ready for independent work · Needs pairing · Second sprint. Add notes for the trainee's record.

4

Print to PDF and update the JSON

Print the rubric for the trainee's file, then update progress/<name>.json with the assessment results and commit.

Trainer Principles

Your role is to unblock, not to teach. The story and labs do the teaching.

Daily check-in

One question: "What broke today? How did you debug it?" Listen for "it works but I don't know why" — that's where to probe.

The 10-minute rule

Trainees struggle alone for 10 minutes before asking. Productive struggle builds the instinct. Immediate answers prevent it.

"What have you tried?"

Always ask this before giving an answer. It surfaces gaps and forces them to articulate their mental model.

Watch the AI usage

If a trainee can't explain code line-by-line, they leaned on AI as a vending machine. Send them back to the lab.
