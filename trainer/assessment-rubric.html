Part 2 — Technical Depth (Probe Questions)
🐧

Linux & Shell

Days 1–2 · Server diagnosis, permissions, networking
— ▾
L1Process & log diagnosis
Ask the trainee:
"Walk me through exactly what you did when you first SSH'd into the crashing server. What commands did you run, in what order, and why?"
Follow up: "What's the difference between kill and kill -9? When would you use each?"
L2Networking on the server
Ask the trainee:
"How did you verify that PostgreSQL was no longer reachable from the internet after you made the change?"
Follow up: "If a new service needs to listen on a port, what's your process for deciding whether to expose it publicly?"
🐳

Docker & Containers

Day 3 · Dockerfile, multi-stage, Compose
— ▾
D1Dockerfile quality
Ask the trainee (show their actual Dockerfile on screen):
"Walk me through why you structured the Dockerfile this way. Why does the order of these instructions matter?"
Follow up: "If I added a new environment variable, which layer cache would be invalidated? How does that affect build time?"
D2Container debugging
Ask the trainee:
"If a container exits immediately after docker run with no output, what are your first three steps?"
Follow up: "How do you get a shell inside a container that's currently running? What about one that already exited?"
☸️

Kubernetes & Helm

Days 4–5 · Deployments, probes, Helm charts, environments
— ▾
K1Kubernetes self-healing & rollouts
Ask the trainee (they should show this live):
"Delete one of your running pods right now. Tell me what's happening as you watch it."
Follow up: "What command would you run to roll back the last deployment? How long does it take? How do you verify it completed?"
K2Probes & production readiness
Ask the trainee:
"What happens to your application's traffic if the readiness probe fails? What if the liveness probe fails?"
Follow up: "You're deploying a new version. Half the pods are up with v2, half still on v1. Is the app taking traffic? From which version?"
K3Helm & environment management
Ask the trainee:
"Show me how you deploy to staging vs production using your Helm chart. What's different between the two? What's shared?"
Follow up: "If Sara accidentally runs helm install ecotrack-prod instead of ecotrack-staging, what happens? How have you protected against that?"
☁️

Cloud & Security

Day 6 · VPC, IAM, S3, least privilege
— ▾
C1IAM & least privilege
Ask the trainee:
"Your CI/CD pipeline needs to push a Docker image to ECR and deploy to EKS. What IAM permissions does it need? How would you set that up?"
Follow up: "A developer asks you for IAM admin access 'just temporarily to debug something.' What do you say and do?"
C2Network architecture
Ask the trainee:
"Walk me through your VPC design. Which resources are in public subnets, which are in private? Why?"
Follow up: "How does a private EC2 instance access the internet to download updates, if it has no public IP?"
🏗️

Terraform & IaC

Day 7 · State, modules, drift, remote backend
— ▾
T1State & drift awareness
Ask the trainee:
"I went into the AWS console and manually changed the instance type of one of your EC2 instances. What happens when you run terraform plan?"
Follow up: "Why is the state file important? What would break if you lost it? Where is yours stored and why?"
T2Modules & reproducibility
Ask the trainee:
"James deleted staging again. Show me how you rebuild it from your Terraform code. How long does it take?"
Follow up: "How are your staging and production environments different in the code? What's shared in the module, what's in the values?"
⚙️

CI/CD Pipeline

Day 8 · GitHub Actions, deploy flow, safety gates
— ▾
P1Pipeline design & safety
Ask the trainee:
"Walk me through what happens from the moment I merge a PR to when it's live in production. Every step."
Follow up: "How would you prevent two engineers from deploying at the same time and causing a conflict?"
P2Failure handling
Ask the trainee (demonstrate live):
"Break a test on purpose and push. Show me what happens in the pipeline and how you'd debug it."
Follow up: "A deploy succeeded in CI but the app is behaving wrongly in production. What's your rollback plan?"
📊

Monitoring & Observability

Day 9 · Prometheus, Grafana, alerts, incident response
— ▾
M1Dashboard interpretation
Ask the trainee (Grafana open on screen):
"Point to each panel and tell me: what is it measuring, what's the unit, and what would make you worried about this graph?"
Follow up: "Your p99 latency is 3x the normal value but error rate is still zero. What could cause that? What do you check first?"
M2Alert quality
Ask the trainee:
"Walk me through one of your alerts — what threshold did you set and why that number specifically?"
Follow up: "Your alert fires at 3am. What's the first thing in your runbook? How long would it take you to find the root cause from the alert alone?"
🧠

Problem-Solving Mindset

How they think under pressure, not just what they know
— ▾
PS1Hypothesis-first debugging
Ask the trainee:
"When you found the performance issue in the capstone, what was your first hypothesis? How did you test it? Were you right?"
Watch for: Do they describe forming a hypothesis before touching anything? Or do they describe randomly trying things?
PS2Knowing the limits of their knowledge
Ask the trainee:
"What's the one thing in this sprint you feel least confident about? What would you do if that became a production issue tomorrow?"
Watch for: Honest self-assessment is a green flag. Claiming to know everything is a red flag.
PS3Learning from the story
Ask the trainee (the final question):
"If you were rebuilding EcoTrack's infrastructure from scratch today — knowing everything you know now — what would you do differently from Day 1?"
Watch for: Specific, opinionated answers show genuine understanding. Vague answers show surface-level learning.
