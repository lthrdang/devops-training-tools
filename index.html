AI Usage Ground Rules

Use AI throughout — but as a senior engineer who asks you guiding questions, not as a solution vending machine.

✓ Use AI to

  • Explain an error you don't understand
  • Review your code and explain the issues
  • Quiz you at the end of a day
  • Simulate an incident for you to diagnose
  • Generate boilerplate you then modify

✗ Don't use AI to

  • Get the answer without understanding it
  • Write code you can't explain line by line
  • Skip the hands-on lab
  • Do the diagnosis for you
  • Generate and submit without reading

The 10-minute rule: struggle alone for 10 minutes before asking. Productive struggle builds the instinct.
